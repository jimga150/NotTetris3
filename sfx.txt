block turn: played when a block is rotated
block move: played when a block has lateral movement

block fall: played when a block goes into freefall and NO lines were cleared
line clear: played when < 4 lines are cleared
4line clear: yeah

gameover1: game over on 1 player games
gameover2: game over on 2 player games

pausesound: played at the moment the game is paused

highscorebeep: played every time letter is entered in high score name entry

new level: played at the moment the game continues after a line clear, IF that line clear put you into the next "level"
